    • About InSearchOfAverage

    InSearchOfAverage is a statistics blog dedicated to providing tutorials, practical applications, and programming guides for learners of all levels. Our mission is to make statistics accessible and understandable for everyone.

    We cover topics such as descriptive statistics, probability, hypothesis testing, data visualization, and statistical software tutorials using R and Python. Whether you are a student, researcher, or professional, you'll find resources here to strengthen your statistical knowledge.
